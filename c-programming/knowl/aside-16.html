Video Description.

  • Different prefix conventions exist for computers, including the following:
  • Kilo, Mega, Giga, Tera, Peta, Exa, Zetta, Yotta
  • Kibi, Mebi, Gibi, Tebi, Pebi, Exbi, Zebi, Yobi
  • the "Apple Convention"
  • For example, a kilobyte (KB) is 1024 (\(2^{10}\)) bytes, but in Apple Convention, it is 1000 (\(10^3\)) bytes
