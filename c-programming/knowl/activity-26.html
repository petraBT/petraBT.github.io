Activity 9.1.

Write a C-program that reads from the user the hour of arrival (between 0 - meaning noon - and 12 - meaning midnight) and that then outputs the price to pay for the night.

The cost for the night is a $10 base fee plus $5 for every hour you arrive past noon. So if you arrive at noon, the total cost is only $10, if you arrive at 1pm, the price goes up to $15, etc. Thankfully the total price is capped at $57, so even if you arrive at 11pm, the price will be $57.
